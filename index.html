Piqueserver is here.

Ace of Spades Classic lives on in this open-source reimplementation of the game’s server.

Find out more

The way Ace of Spades was intended.

Many people loved the 0.75 version of the classic Ace of Spades. This reimplementation of the server ensures that the game lives on for people to enjoy with new improvements, while staying authentic to the original game.

Get started!

Why piqueserver?

Authentic

Piqueserver is compatible with Ace of Spades 0.75 clients, including OpenSpades.

Stable

Mature codebase provides reliable gameplay, modding capabilies, and extensive documentation.

Modern

A continuation of PySnip, piqueserver runs on modern systems with Python 3.

Free

No cost and GPLv3, piqueserver is free for enjoyment and improvement. Contributions welcome!

Voxel FPS

Ace of Spades is a block-based first person shooter, and piqueserver is an open-source version of the classic Ace of Spades 0.75 server.

  • Client: OpenSpades
  • Date: 2019-11-16

Build or Destroy

Besides shooting, gameplay involves building or destroying blocks to your advantage - whether it be building fortifications to hide behind or sneaking up on your enemy via tunnels.

  • Client: OpenSpades
  • Date: 2019-11-16

Attack!

Featuring multiple modes of play (including Capture the Flag, Arena, and Tug of War), piqueserver offers many map and script (mod) options. In all cases, however, eliminate the enemy!

  • Client: OpenSpades
  • Date: 2019-11-16

Grenade!

When playing on piqueserver, weapon choice can be critical: take long, well placed shots with the rifle, or provide cover fire with the SMG. Use the shotgun or grenades to quickly clear rooms.

  • Client: OpenSpades
  • Date: 2019-11-16

Configuration is Easy

Leveraging TOML configuration files, piqueserver is easy to configure. Extensive commenting allows new users to get their server running to their liking in no time! Stuck? Read the extensive documentation or ask the vibrant community for help!

  • Date: 2019-11-16

Runs Remotely

It is easy to run piqueserver remotely through the included SSH utility. Piqueserver is started from the command line, making it easy to incorporate into scripts.

  • Date: 2019-11-16
